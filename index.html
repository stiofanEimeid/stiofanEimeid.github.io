About Me

My name is Stephen Byrne. I live in Dublin, Ireland and I am learning how to be a software developer. This is my website.

Timeline

  • 1992: Born 10th June.

  • 2010: Finish Secondary School and join the Art, Design and Three-Dimensional Studios Portfolio Preparation Course at Ballyfermot College of Further Education.

  • 2011:Complete course at Ballyfermot before beginning work as a barista for nine months.

  • 2013: Resit my Leaving Certificate and gain entry to the Law with Social Justice programme at University College Dublin (UCD).

  • 2016: Complete a semester abroad as part of the Erasmus Exchange Programme in Toulouse, France. Complete a two-month summer internship at the Irish Penal Reform Trust, a Dublin-based NGO that campaigns for the respect for the rights of prisoners and the use of prison as a last resort.

  • 2017: Graduate from UCD with a 2:1. Begin studying the LLM in Human Rights Law at the University of Nottingham.

  • 2018: Compete in the Philip C. Jessup International Law Moot Competition. Graduate from the University of Nottingham with a 2:1. Begin to learn how to code and complete Founders and Coders prerequisites.
